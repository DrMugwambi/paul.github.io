About Us

NEHO ORGANICS Traditional Medicine Hospital is an African ancient healing system with a rich history spanning thousands of years, deeply rooted in African philosophy. This holistic approach reflects a profound understanding of the interconnectedness of health and spirituality within the framework of traditional practices.
NEHO ORGANICS born through Founder and owner Registered Zimbabwe Traditional Medical Practitioner Patronela Thandiwe Warikandwa. The call came after hundreds of people were assisted under her care who suffered from different ailments and in some instances severe and life threatening securing full recovery. Neho Organics uses natural methods for health and recovery and compliment conventional medicine practices.
